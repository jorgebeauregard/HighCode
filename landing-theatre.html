our story

Brave Theatre is a nationwide leader in the creation and presentation of new, cutting-edge theatrical works and in promoting its ensemble-based theatrical techniques.

Our team of actors, directors, sound engineers, costume designers, and just creative people is guided by an artistic vision centered on the core values of collaboration, transformation, and invention. We seek to capture audiences’ imaginations leaving them charged and empowered.

reviews

“Beatiful Queen” was one of the best written, directed, acted and structured projects of this sort that I have seen to date. With this already being the fourth show at your theatre, my final verdict would be that this particular new writing night is one to watch and keep on watching!

Philip Lawrence

Theatrical Critic

Beautiful Queen is one of the most socially important and perfectly done performances put on by the team of Brave Theatre. They are rapidly growing as one of the strongest short theatres. They are consistent in both quantity and quality and with this being the tenth successful one it looks set to continue for a good while.

Debra Ortega

Journalist

I was delighted to be involved in writing script for your version of my story “Diamond Earrings”. It was a fantastic opportunity for me to get my work seen in front of an encouraging and supportive audience. Direction and casting of my play was absolutely perfect, I couldn't have asked for more. I wish you all the best for the future.

Jose Gutierrez

Writer
